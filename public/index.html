FinLit Pro — Free Financial Literacy for Students & Beginners

FinLit Pro is a free, interactive financial-literacy app that teaches investing, stock markets, valuation metrics, asset allocation, mutual funds and ETFs, and personal money management. It includes a searchable glossary of 100+ finance terms, live market data and charts, an AI market predictor, and an AI financial advisor.

Topics covered: how to start investing, reading financial statements, RSI and MACD indicators, diversification, risk and return, compound interest, budgeting, and more.
